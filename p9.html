|  Project 09
Consentful Interface

|  Description

This is a simulation of my dog's life. Dogs have poor vision. How the world looks like in the eyes of dog is very different from how we see the world. In this project, I simulate the vision of dogs. In certain times, there will be a goal to look for based on my dog's living habit.
Audiences who don't allow camera access will see a pre-set image that changes over time, instead of their cameras' views.

|  Reflection

Often times say no to sharing your privacy means not being able to access the service. In this project, I'd like to create a camera-based dog vision simulator that allow audiences to enjoy it even if they are not comfortable with using camera.
